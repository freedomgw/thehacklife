About Me

Hey! Thanks for checking my website! I am interested in mobile and web development ranging from the front-end to the back-end development.

Like most high school students, I wasn't entirely sure of who I wanted to be when I finished my study career. However, I knew from the start that I was interested in creating things and I had little experience in programming. In the end, I chose computer science in one of the most prestigious school that Canada has to offer, University of Waterloo.

I am a fourth year computer science student from the University of Waterloo in a five year co-operative program. During my studies in the University of Waterloo, I have learned that I have growing passion in creating software technology. These experiences were found through my five internships in both Canada and United States.

I am excited to see what's in store in the future! In the meantime, I will keep coding away and will be blogging interesting experiences that I have found on my website.
